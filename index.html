A note from the medical director

IM Clinic is my vision for the ideal primary care clinic. IM stands for internal medicine- the specialty I have trained in through my academic career and post-graduate medical training. I truly believe in person-focused (not disease-oriented) care over time.

We are in the midst of drastic changes in the landscape of healthcare in America. What the future holds, only time will tell. One thing is certain: my goal is to provide the highest level of care for my patients -- regardless of the changing landscape of healthcare.

I aspire to bring evidence-based medicine, care and compassion, and technology to the forefront of this medical practice.

I look forward to being your partner in this journey of health!

Sincerely,
Dr. Omer Iqbal
